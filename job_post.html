Research Associate

Winter Research Internship Program is designed to stimulate undergraduate and masters students to undergo research projects under LCS2. The duration of the internship is at least 8 weeks, between December and February. The internship is available to external candidates only, from any Indian University/ Institute. The internship will be paid.

Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria: Students pursuing a Bachelor’s (2nd/3rd year, 3rd year preferred) and Master’s degree program are eligible to apply.

Application Procedure

The application includes:

  1. A cover letter summarising the application (including research interests), and a summary of a few papers published by LCS2 which are relevant to the application.
  2. Recent CV including the CGPA and any ML/NLP projects
  3. A copy of photo ID (proof for name given in CV).

Selection Process: The LCS2 members will review the submitted candidates’ applications. Selected candidates will undergo a few rounds of interviews, including coding and face to face interview (online)
