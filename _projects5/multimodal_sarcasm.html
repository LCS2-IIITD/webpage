Multimodal Sarcasm Analysis

Overview

Majority of the times users use sarcasm as a means of mocking or poking fun on social media. This, however, isn't apparent easily and is even difficult if one looks only at the text. If we take into consideration the image associated with the text the task becomes easier since it may provide us with some context of the situation under focus. Thus, our aim is to use multiple modalities (i.e text and image) to generate the corresponding non-sarcastic utterance of the sarcastic text.
