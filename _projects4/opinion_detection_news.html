Opinion Detection in News

Overview

Humans like to express their opinions and crave the opinions of others. Mining and detection of opinions from a variety of sources are beneficial to individuals, organisations, and even governments. One such organisation is Media, where a general norm is not to showcase opinions from their side. And as anchors are the face of media, it is required for them not to be opinionated. Thus, we are building upon a model which could detect opinions in news delivery so that it is helpful for anchors and media to reflect upon their work and hence improve upon their delivery.
